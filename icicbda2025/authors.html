Manuscript Submission

  • ICICBDA 2025 follows the DOUBLE-BLIND peer review system, hence authors are informed not to disclose their identity like their names, affiliations, acknowledgments, etc. in the initial submission of the paper.
  • Authors are invited to submit their original research papers including results, tables, figures, and references in PDF format using the following Submission Link
  • Authors are advised to prepare their papers according to the Springer CCIS formatting guidelines (Minimum 12 pages and to maximum of 15 pages (including proposed methodology to solve the problem, research findings, results, and references), around 400 words on each page should be written in English and in Word document /LaTeX).
  • The similarity check for submitted papers will be carried out. Similarity ratio of the papers should not exceed 10% overall, and 3% for single source overlap. .
  • The submissions not fulfilling above two criterion will be sent back to authors and will not be considered for review.
